Research Experiences

  • Research on the dilemma and development methods of the county-level media center
    As the leader of the project, I organized our members to take the opportunity of the internship to go to nine county-level media centers to conduct on-the-spot research and in-depth interviews.
  • A field study of the dual-track model of using live broadcasting to help farmers during the epidemic
    We visited several townships and counties in Anhui Province and conduct research, analyzed the development path and existing problems of using live broadcasting to help farmers, and fed back our research report to the local government.
  • Research on the employment and entrepreneurship of the disabled under the background of digital technology empowerment
    We cooperated with the Qixia District Disabled Persons' Federation and provided practical suggestions for helping the disabled to re-employ in the digital age through multi-party visits, research and data search.
  • The 3rd Yogyakarta Communication Conference
    I made a speech on the innovative diffusion of live-streaming agricultural assistance models in rural areas under the background of the epidemic.
